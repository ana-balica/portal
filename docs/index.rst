Welcome to Systers Portal's documentation!
==========================================

Systers Portal is a unified platform for Systers and its sub-groups to share
information and get the latest news. It solve the communication and interaction
issue between groups.

Reasons to create Systers Portal
--------------------------------

Systers, along with other sub-groups, have several channels where information is
disseminated. A portal for Systers and sub-groups to share information to
perspective communities, lists events and by geography. Before rebranding and
new website, ABI (Anita Borg Institute) had a section/pages for Systers and
each subgroup (Latinas, LGBT, Black Women in Computing, TurkishWiC and ArabWiC).
This allowed for subgroup members to find information specific to their groups.
